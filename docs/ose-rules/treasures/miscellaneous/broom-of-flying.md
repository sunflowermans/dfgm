# Broom of Flying
{: .no_toc }

A broom that can be commanded to fly, carrying the owner aloft.

- **Speed:** The broom flies at 240’ (80’).
- **Passengers:** An additional passenger may be carried. When carrying two people, the broom’s speed is 180’ (60’).
