# Know Alignment
{: .no_toc }

*2nd Level Cleric Spell*

**Duration:** 1 round

**Range:** 10’

Reveals the alignment of one character, monster, object, or area within range. (Most objects or areas do not have an alignment, but magic items or holy places may.)
