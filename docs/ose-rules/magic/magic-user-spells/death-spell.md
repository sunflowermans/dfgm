# Death Spell
{: .no_toc }

*6th Level Magic-User Spell*

**Duration:** Instant

**Range:** 240’

Up to 4d8 Hit Dice of creatures within a 60’ cube area must **save versus death** or die, instantly.

**Restrictions:** Undead and creatures with greater than 7 Hit Dice are unaffected.
